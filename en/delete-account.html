Delete Your Account

In accordance with Article 17 GDPR (right to erasure)

You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time, in two ways:

Option 1 — Directly in the app

Open the app → Settings → Account → Delete my account. This method is immediate and does not require waiting for an email.

Option 2 — Via this page (if you no longer have access to the app)

Enter the email address associated with your account. You will receive a confirmation link valid for 24 hours.

[Technical note: this form must be connected to the backend endpoint that generates a signed token and sends the confirmation email via Supabase Auth]

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

After confirmation, the following are permanently deleted:

  • Your account and user identifier
  • All generated and saved stories
  • Your favourites and narrative preferences
  • Your generated audio files
  • Your subscription information (an active subscription must be cancelled separately via Google Play / App Store)
⚠️ This action is irreversible. If you have an active paid subscription, remember to cancel it separately through your Google Play or App Store account — account deletion does not automatically stop recurring billing on the store side.

Processing Time

Deletion takes effect immediately after confirming the link received by email (or immediately if performed directly within the app).
